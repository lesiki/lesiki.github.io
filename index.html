About me

I'm currently Senior Software Developer at FrontlineSMS, where I work with a cool team of devs and other techies on a Groovy/Grails app for SMS-based automation, as well as a complementary Android app. I'm a Full Stack dev in a team that does DevOps.

Prior to my time at Frontline, I worked at CGI (formerly Logica), as a middleware software dev on a long-running SOA-based project in the private telecommunications sector.

I have a Masters in Computer Engineering from the University of Manchester. During my Uni years, I did a few internships, the longest being a 10-week placement at Deutsche Bank in London, where I helped automate in-house processes in their Liquidity Risk IT team.
