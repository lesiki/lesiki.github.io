About me

I am co-founder at Hodi, where we build custom tech that empowers our SDR team to do world-class remote B2B sales.

From 2018 to 2021, I was CTO at Pula, who insure over a million smallholder farmers. Pula's core tech stack is in Python + Django running on AWS, with mobile apps built in Kotlin and Dart + Flutter

I co-founded AfricanStockPhoto, where we help photographers from Africa tell more authentic stories. I built our system in Groovy/Grails, with touches of Vue, Java Spark, React and NodeJS.

Until early 2018 I was CTO at FrontlineSMS, an awesome messaging automation startup where I led the full-stack dev team responsible for designing, building, testing and running everything at Frontline.

Prior to my time at Frontline, I worked at CGI (formerly Logica), as a middleware software dev on a long-running SOA-based project in the private telecommunications sector.

I got my Masters in Computer Engineering from the University of Manchester, during which time I did placement at Deutsche Bank in London, where I helped automate in-house processes in their Liquidity Risk IT team.
